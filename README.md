A playful, single-page web app that assigns you a **ridiculous life destiny** based on your favorite snack.

Completely client-side. No backend. No tracking. Just snacks and fate.

---

## Features

- **Digital fortune cookie** with animation + crack sound
- Enter any snack to personalize your destiny
- **500+ absurd life paths**, generated dynamically
- **Rare Destiny System**
  - Common (60%)
  - Uncommon (25%)
  - Rare (10%)
  - Legendary (4%)
  - Mythic (1%)
- Visual effects for rare & mythic destinies
- **Shareable fortune links** (URL-encoded)
- Share to **X/Twitter**, **Facebook**, or copy link
- Mobile-friendly, responsive design
- Works fully offline (except social sharing)
